Hi there, nice to see you,
my name is Laurens.

Awesome! You have reached my corner of the internet, and you took the effort to click on the About link to learn more about who is doing all the writing and stuff here.

Mainly I like to learn and become better at what I enjoy doing. I try to spend most of my days doing exactly that, and drinking preferably good coffee ☕️ while at it. My personality is characterized by my determination and my desire to find the true meaning behind every statement. I have a strong intrinsic motivation to make the most out of everything I take on. I am an energetic, creative and analytical thinker, I have experienced that I function best when I can see progress in the work I take on. I'm heavily into sports and my work: both I like to do in teams. In my experience, the group dynamics and the surplus of the team as a whole determine its success. I always look for ways to improve myself and the people around me. I guess for me it is an art to keep a balance in everything I do.

Interests

Psychology.

Clinical-, neuro-, applied-, and sportspsychology. It has been my major focus during my studies and my first occupation after I graduated.

Tech.

Technology is awesome. I like to use tech and read all about it. I spent way too much time refreshing 'The Verge' for news.

Health.

Whenever you're healthy you don't think about it, only when your health is fading you start to notice how imporant it is.

Design.

I can get very excited about good Mobile and Web design. But also architecture and interior design.

Sports.

Everything with a ball, but also strength and conditioning, running... as long as it gets my heart to pump faster.

Development.

In everything I do I need to see and experience developement to stay interested. "Stilstand is achteruitgang."

Work

Product Owner at Sense Health

2015 - currently

Currently I mainly focus on our services in the field of behaviour activation and smart coaching. As a product owner I often find myself managing expectations, grooming our backlog and having meetings with all kinds of stakeholders and users. It can be quite a challenge to maintain the pace of development and safeguard the quality of the products that we deliver. At Sense I got the opportunity to grow the skills needed to be a product owner, but also picked up a lot of important technical skills. I learned a lot about e.g. data structures, (software-) development, analytics and quality control. I'm proud of our accomplishment to initiate a truly new concept of delivering (mental) health care: need driven care. To put this into practice we have started a completely new division, together with Parnassia Groep, called "Digitale Poli".

Psychologist and manager at Forta Groep

2011 - 2015

Previously I have worked as a psychologist at a mental health care company. A challenging position as it was my first professional job. Dealing with people with all kinds of mental illnesses, like depression, stress and anxiety disorders. At Forta Groep I have also started and grown a new department for the company that focussed on supporting general practitioners by providing them with a psychologist in the office. Strong social- and communicative skills are among the most important characteristics I've got to develop during my period at Forta Groep. I'm most proud of starting and growing a new department up to 50+ psychologists that work in general practitioners' offices.

Education

Leiden University and University of Wales, Bangor

2005 - 2011

At Leiden University I learned a lot of stuff about Psychology, how to work in teams effectively and my fair share of critical thinking. Masters in Clinical and Cognitive Psychology.

At Bangor I took a completely different route with theater and American TV culture classes. On the Psychology front I mainly focused on the consumer-side of Psychology. Learned about consumers and what motivates them.
